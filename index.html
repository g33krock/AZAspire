AZ Aspire Academy - Inspiring Excellence

Our Mission - Our priority is to ensure your child is passionate about learning. The teachers, administrators and staff at AZ Aspire Academy provide comprehensive one-to-one and small group education on your terms. We are united in the belief that all children must have self esteem, necessary resources, and a nurturing environment in order to succeed.

Our unique program is suited for students with ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorders, Asperger's, Anxiety, Dyslexia, Giftedness, Medical Fragility, and Student Athletes and Professionals with Alternative Scheduling Issues. Students have a 1:1 class size for academic courses and a 6:1 for elective courses; STEAM, Yoga, PE, Music, and Art.
